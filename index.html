About Srov

Empowering Cambodia's Agricultural Community

Srov is a digital platform designed to empower farmers by helping them sell their products and agricultural equipment, connect with buyers, and trade with other farmers easily.

Our goal is to create a trusted online community that supports local farmers and simplifies agricultural trading and communication.

Whether you're looking to sell your harvest, find quality equipment, or connect with other farmers, Srov provides all the tools you need in one convenient platform.

Why Choose Srov?

Everything you need to succeed in agricultural trading

Sell Farm Products Easily

Upload photos, set prices, and list your agricultural products in minutes. Reach buyers directly and negotiate terms quickly.

Buy or Trade Equipment

Browse verified listings of farm machinery and equipment. Trade with other farmers or purchase from trusted sellers.

Contact and Negotiate Directly

Built-in messaging system lets you communicate with farmers and buyers. Negotiate prices and terms in real-time.

Track and Manage Sales

Monitor your listings, track inquiries, and manage your sales history. Stay organized with our easy-to-use dashboard.

How It Works

Get started in four simple steps

Register

Create your account in minutes. Join our growing community of farmers and buyers.

Post Product

Upload photos and details of what you want to sell or trade. Add pricing and location information.

Connect

Receive inquiries from interested buyers or browse available products from other farmers.

Trade or Sell

Negotiate terms, finalize the deal, and complete your transaction successfully.
